OVERVIEW
Provides support and maintenance of the Financial Information Systems (FIS) in addition to
other systems supported by the Accounting & Finance teams. The Finance Business Analyst serves
as a technical point-of-contact and assists subject matter experts with ensuring data integrity,
testing of system changes, report writing, data analytics and identifying process improvement
opportunities. The Finance Business Analyst also supports FIS upgrades, patches, testing and
other technical projects as assigned.

QUALIFICATIONS
Education: Bachelor's Degree: Finance or Accounting
Work Experience: Information systems; Finance; Accountant

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Workday experience preferred.
- Generates standard and ad hoc reports and Finance metrics, and continuously research, review,
  compile, and analyze financial data.
- Provide support for Accounting & Finance including, but not limited to, researching and
  resolving FIS problems, unexpected results or process flaws; perform scheduled activities,
  reports and audits; recommend solutions or alternate methods to meet requirements.
- Maintain Accounting and Finance and affiliated system or database tables and system
  set up/configuration.
- Provide liaison support between functional end users, external vendors and local IT, assisting
  in the development of tools and programs that meet user expectations within system capabilities.
- Assist in the review, testing and implementation of FIS system upgrades or patches. Collaborate
  with functional and technical staff to coordinate application of upgrade or fix. Document
  process and results.

COMPENSATION
$33.72/hour - $58.00/hour (good-faith range at time of posting). Annualizes to roughly
$70,140 - $120,640 at 40 hours/week. Comprehensive benefits package.
